Getting started

1. Add a supplier on the Suppliers page.

2. Register a product against that supplier on the Products page — this also writes the first ledger entry.

3. Log shipment checkpoints on the Track Shipment page as the product moves.

4. Anyone can confirm authenticity on the public Verify Product page.
